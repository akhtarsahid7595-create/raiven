Your Daily 20-Minute Ritual

5-10 minutes each. High-yield psychological investment setup.

☀️ MORNING (5-10 MIN)

Step 1: Write your daily goal

Example: "Don't defend myself if criticized."

Step 2: Identify your potential trigger

Example: "Boss might criticize → I'll pause and listen."

Step 3: Check your energy

Example: "I'm tired. I'll be extra careful about reacting."

🌙 EVENING (5-10 MIN)

Step 1: Did I accomplish my goal?

Honest answer required: Yes / Partial / No.

Step 2: Celebrate your silent win

Example: "Someone disrespected me. I paused. Didn't blow up."

Step 3: Learn from issues

Example: "Reacted defensively. Next time I'll ask for clarity."

Step 4: Set tomorrow's focus

Example: "Stay calm in conversations."

TOTAL TIME: 20 Minutes Per Day.
